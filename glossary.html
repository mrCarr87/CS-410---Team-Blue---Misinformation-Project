Glossary

Algorithm

A process or set of rules to be followed in calculations or other problem solving operations

Disinformation

False or inaccurate information, often shared with intent of causing harm.

Machine Learning (ML)

A subsection of AI that is focused on specific algorithms that have the capability to learn from various types and patterns of training data in order to accurately predict regarding new data.

Misinformation

False or inaccurate information, often shared without the intent of causing harm.

Natural Language Processing (NLP)

A subsection of AI that allows computers to understand and generate human language.
