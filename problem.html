Problem

Given the spread of information due to easy access to social media, the spread of misinformation now targets a range of topics such as politics, business, and everyday life. Trust in mass media has fallen drastically in recent years.

Problem Characteristics

  • Average readers lack the tools to easily verify information.
  • The scale of online content makes manual checks extremely difficult.
  • Only 34% of Americans trust mass media to report in an unbiased, factual manner (Gallup, 2022).
  • Misinformation spreads up to ten times faster than credible reporting on social media (PIRG, 2025).
